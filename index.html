The α7 has two forms of wireless sharing capability built in; NFC One-touch allows image transfer with a single tap to any NFC-compatible smartphone, tablet, or laptop. Wi-Fi® allows quick uploading from many locations.
Megapixels
The 36 Megapixel Full-Frame sensor found inside the A7r is not just an incredibly high resolution, but is also used without the conventional Optical Low-Pass Filter. Usually put in place to prevent moiré caused by strong repeating patterns, removing this filter allows the sensor to capture an even greater level of detail.
Сontrols
Catering more towards enthusiasts, the body is mostly magnesium-alloy for a quality feel, with dual control dials for easy access to manual controls. The big, bright electronic viewfinder has a resolution of around 2.5 million dots, displaying a brilliantly sharp image with no noticeable delay. Intuitive, direct-access menus mean you won't be delayed while changing camera settings either.
Important Details
Combined with the extra detail and clarity that is inherent with Full-Frame sensors, the Sony A7r is more than capable of delivering results to satisfy even the most demanding photographers.Like most new cameras the A7r offers wireless remote control and image transfer via a compatible smartphone, with NFC capability making the connection process fast and simple. With a quick tap, remote viewing and shooting is only seconds away.
